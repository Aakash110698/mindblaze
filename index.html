Technical quiz Rules

  • In the Round-I 40 Questions will be displayed on the Projector screen.
  • Each question will be displayed for nearly 1 min (60 seconds),at the same time one organizer will read the question.
  • Each question carries 1 point.
  • It is a pen and paper based, the Participants will write their answers on the paper.
  • The answer sheet will be collected by the organizers and will be validated properly.
  • Selected teams will be sent for Second Round.
  • Those teams who are not selected can attend other events.
  • The answers for the questions will be discussed after the 5 minutes break by our organizer.(Timing-11:00 to 11:15AM)
  • Finally, the result for the Round-I will be declared after discussing the answer.
  • Participants are selected on the basis of their earned points in the quiz.
